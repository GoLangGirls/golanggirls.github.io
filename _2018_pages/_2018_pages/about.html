Vision

Our aim is to give tools and a community for women to understand GoLang. We do this by providing a great experience on building things and by making technology more approachable. GoLangGirls is born in Pune, non-profit volunteer community.

Mission

We want this event to be global. If you wish to host event in your city contact us on golanggirlspune@gmail.com
